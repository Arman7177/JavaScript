THE BAND

We love music and playing live!

SRBUK

Srbuk blends elements of soul, pop, jazz, and R&B, which makes her performances both modern and emotional. Her performances often convey a strong inner energy and sincerity, creating a style that deeply touches the listener’s emotions.

DJ MILL

DJ Mill is a contemporary Armenian DJ known for his energetic, stylish, and artistic approach. He stands out with his refined taste in music selection, mastery of both vinyl and digital techniques, and his ability to create an intense and memorable atmosphere on both club and festival stages.

DAVID GREG

David Greg is an Armenian singer who stands out with his modern sound, delicate vocals, and expressive emotions. His music blends elements of pop, indie, and electronic genres, creating an emotional, often melancholic, yet fresh and light-filled sound.

KAMI FRIENDS

Kami Friends Project is an Armenian music group known for its multi-layered, unconventional, and deeply emotional compositions. The band blends elements of contemporary and alternative music, creating a unique sound that transcends traditional genre boundaries.

DJ THINK

DJ Think blends techno, minimal, house, and at times experimental and ambient sounds, creating deep, atmospheric, and often meditative layers. He is especially recognized for the “sense of flow” in his sets—smooth transitions, rhythmic structure, and a subtle play between anticipation and release.
